ABOUT US



Founded in the early 1950s by Late Sheikh Mahmood Saleh Abbar, the Company was formally
incorporated as a 100% Saudi Joint Liability partnership after Sheikh Mahmood’s death in 1400H
(1980G). With its Head Office in Jeddah, the Company is organized in a standard Industrial
configuration supported by highly experienced and qualified personnel controlling and monitoring
branch operations in Makkah, Madinah, Riyadh, Dammam,, and Khamis Mushayt.

The operations are enhanced through fully equipped and professionally staffed service facilities,
which are an additional asset towards ABBAR’S reliability and versatility. ABBAR’S network
of branches and offices provides customers small and large through out the Kingdom with on-the
spot service. ales and marketing data are fed back to ABBAR’S Head Office in Jeddah for
computer analysis and serve as a basis for efficient forward planning to ensure that ABBAR is
constantly in touch with customer’s demands.

The Company’s distribution network has been strengthened by modern warehousing, major new
repair facilities and numerous new retails outlets; today, ABBAR provides comprehensive
distribution throughout the Kingdom of an ever increasing range of domestic and commercial
appliances, supported by customers servicing which ranks among the best in Saudi Arabia.
